# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Research Objective

The objective of this project is to understand how leading practitioners use LinkedIn as an organic growth channel to build audiences, establish authority, generate demand, and influence business outcomes in B2B environments.

This research focuses on identifying repeatable frameworks, content patterns, and growth strategies that can be applied by growth marketers and early-stage B2B companies.

# Expert Selection Criteria

Experts were selected based on the following criteria:

* Demonstrated success using LinkedIn as a primary content distribution channel.
* Relevance to B2B marketing, SaaS, founder-led growth, or audience building.
* Consistent publication of educational content.
* Practical frameworks that can be adapted by growth marketers.
* Publicly available content that allows for qualitative analysis.

# Research Methodology

This project follows a hybrid research approach designed to balance research quality, technical feasibility, and learning outcomes.

## LinkedIn Content Collection

Recent LinkedIn posts will be manually identified from publicly accessible expert profiles. Manual curation was chosen because LinkedIn provides limited public API access and employs strict anti-scraping measures.

For each expert, selected posts will be documented using the following criteria:

* Educational value.
* Presence of repeatable frameworks or actionable insights.
* Relevance to B2B marketing and audience growth.
* Evidence of strategic thinking rather than motivational content.

For every post analyzed, the following information will be recorded:

* Publication date.
* Original post URL.
* Brief summary.
* Key marketing insight.
* Implications for B2B growth practitioners.

## YouTube Transcript Collection

Selected video transcripts will be collected programmatically using the `youtube-transcript-api` Python library.

The transcript extraction workflow demonstrates the use of APIs and AI-assisted coding tools to automate repetitive research tasks.

The process involves:

1. Identifying high-signal videos relevant to LinkedIn growth and audience building.
2. Extracting transcripts through an API-based workflow.
3. Storing transcripts in structured markdown files.
4. Synthesizing recurring themes across experts.

Only videos that provide strategic insights, frameworks, or detailed explanations will be included.


# Expected Deliverables

By the completion of this project, the repository will contain:

* Profiles of 10 selected experts and the rationale for their inclusion.
* Analysis of approximately 30 LinkedIn posts (3 posts per expert).
* A curated set of YouTube transcripts collected through APIs.
* Cross-expert patterns and strategic insights relevant to B2B growth.
* Documentation of the research process and methodology.

# Limitations

* LinkedIn content analysis is based on publicly available posts at the time of research.
* The project prioritizes depth of analysis over exhaustive coverage.
* Findings represent qualitative insights derived from selected high-signal content sources.
* YouTube transcripts will only be collected for videos that directly contribute to the research objective.
